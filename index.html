About

South Leeds Board Gamers are a friendly community of board and tabletop game enthusiasts, playing everything from euro to war games and anything in-between.

We invite players of all experiences to discover this wonderful hobby and make lasting friends along the way. Players can either bring their own games or play one of the many games members bring and just jump in - we're always more than happy to teach!

Get Involved

We meet between 7pm and 11pm, weekly at Bruntcliffe Working Men's Club in Morley. The venue has both parking and a bar for drinks on site. Including a takeaway round the corner if you get hungry.

The best way to keep up to date and is to join our Facebook Group for regular updates and events.
